It is 2026 and somehow Apple does not allow users to have natural scrolling on the trackpad and inverse scrolling on the mouse at the same time.

Here are some alternatives:
- Logi Options+
- UnnaturalScrollWheels
- LinearMouse (Preferred)

Logi Options+ was an absolute shitshow when AWS went down and people could not open the damn app because it needed a network connection for... some reason. It also does not work if you do not own a Logitech Mouse compatible with the software. I would not recommend this software.

[UnnaturalScrollWheels](https://github.com/ther0n/UnnaturalScrollWheels) as of current does not have a notarized app that works on macOS Tahoe. If on a previous version of macOS, this is a solid option.

[LinearMouse](https://github.com/linearmouse/linearmouse) is an alternative I found after both of the above options stopped working on macOS Tahoe. It is a great piece of software, and offers more customizability compared to the first two options.
